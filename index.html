Who we are

Organo scope Ltd. is a startup founded and run by a group of professional Kenyan women based in Kenya and the diaspora. The business's operation being breeding of the Black soldier fly (BSF) larvae. The BSF larvae would be sold as animal feed with plans being made to eventually produce organic efficient fertilizers for crop farmers.

Our plans

The activities Organoscope plans to carry out are:

  1. Raising BSF to produce animal feed and organic fertilizers
  2. Training women, youth and small-scale farmers to not only raise BSF but provide a source of income and market for their products
  3. Organic waste collection and management .

Mission and vision

Our mission at Organo Scope Ltd. is to breed BSF as revenue both for the company and the vulnerable community, to create job opportunities for the community, and to reduce the demand for omena as the primary source of protein for animal feed.

Learn more about BSF farming

  • What is BSF?

    BSF(Black Soldier Fly) are the type of flies that are slightly bigger than the normal flies and are known for their extraordinary ability to consume and reduce organic waste. It is a non-pest insect species that can convert diverse (non-value) organic waste streams into biomass which can then be used to produce animal feed.

  • Why BSF?

    At larvae stage BSF can transform organic waste into high quality protein which can be used as alternative protein additive in animal feed.

  • What products do we obtained from BSF?

    • Organo meal
      This is a feed containing 55% protein which can be used for Chickens,fish and Pets(monogastric animals).
    • Organo oil
      This is a pure natural oil with high lauric acid concentration which is used as aquaculture feed, pet food and alternative to fish,coconut and palm oil.
    • Organo soil
      This is an organic soil conditioner prepared from larvae residue containing high nitrogen, phosphorus and potassium which are essential in soil fertility.

  • What is Organo Scope ltd's aim?

    Produce both animal feed and fertilizer in sustainable quantities that will impact affordability and accesability by the breeding of Black Soldier fly using pre-identified waste products.
