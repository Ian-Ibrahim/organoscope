Learn more about BSF farming

  • What is Organo scope aim?

    Produce both animal feed and fertilizer in sustainable quantities that will impact affordability and accesability by the breeding of Black Soldier fly using pre-identified waste products.

  • What is BSF?

    BSF(Black Soldier Fly) are the type of flies that are slightly bigger than the normal flies and are known for their extraordinary ability to consume and reduce organic waste. It is a non-pest insect species that can convert diverse (non-value) organic waste streams into biomass which can then be used to produce animal feed.

  • Why BSF?

    At larvae stage BSF can transform organic waste into high quality protein which can be used as alternative protein additive in animal feed.

  • What products do we obtained from BSF?

    • Organo meal
      This is a feed containing 55% protein which can be used for Chickens,fish and Pets(monogastric animals).
    • Organo oil
      This is a pure natural oild with high lauric acid concentration which is used as aquaculture feed, pet food and alternative to fish,coconut and palm oil.
    • Organo soil
      This is an organic soil conditioner prepared from larval residue containing high nitrogen, phosphorus and potassium which are essential in soil fertility.
